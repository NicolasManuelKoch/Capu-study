How to delete the voice recorder app

After the study, you may wish to remove the voice recorder app from your phone. The instructions below show, step by step, how to delete the app from your home screen on iPhone and Android phones.

Quick steps (summary)

  1. Unlock your phone and find the voice recorder app you used for the study.
  2. Touch and hold the app icon until a small menu or options appear.
  3. Choose the option to Remove, Delete, or Uninstall the app.
  4. Confirm that you want to delete the app when a message appears on screen.

On iPhone or iPad

  1. Find the Voice Recorder app (or the app you used for the study) on your home screen.
  2. Touch and hold the app icon until a menu appears.
  3. Tap Remove App.
  4. Tap Delete App.
  5. Tap Delete again to confirm.

On Android phones

  1. Find the voice recorder app icon on your home screen or in the app drawer.
  2. Touch and hold the app icon.
  3. Drag it to Uninstall, or tap the App info button.
  4. Tap Uninstall or Delete.
  5. Tap OK to confirm.

If you need help at any point, call us at (236)808-4099 and we can guide you through the steps.

What happens when you delete the app?

  • The voice recorder app is removed from your phone, and you will no longer see it on your home screen.
  • Any recordings saved inside the app only may be removed if you delete the app. Recordings you have already backed up elsewhere (for example, sent to the research team or saved to cloud storage) are not affected.
  • If you later decide you want to use a voice recorder again, you can install the same app or a similar one from the App Store or Google Play Store.

For the purposes of this study, you are welcome to delete the voice recorder app once you have finished all tasks. Keeping or deleting the app afterwards is entirely your choice.
