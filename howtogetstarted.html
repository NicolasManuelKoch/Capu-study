How to get started with the ChatGPT app

This first video shows how to open the App Store/Play Store, find ChatGPT, install it, and create or log into your account.

Open in App Store (iPhone/iPad) Open in Google Play (Android)

Quick steps

  1. Open the App Store (iPhone/iPad) or Google Play (Android).
  2. Search for “ChatGPT” and confirm the publisher is OpenAI.
  3. Tap Get / Install.
  4. Open the app, and Sign up or Log in.
  5. Then follow the second video below to personalize ChatGPT and turn off data training.

If you need help, call us at (236)808-4099 and we’ll walk you through it.

This second video shows how to add the necessary memories and how to open Settings to turn off data training (“Improve the model for everyone”) without turning off chat history.

Turn off data training (keep chat history on)

Please follow these steps in the ChatGPT app so your study chats are not used to train the model. You will still be able to see your past chats; we are only turning off the option that sends data for training.

  1. Open the ChatGPT app.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) or your profile icon, then choose Settings.
  3. Go to Data Controls.
  4. Do not turn off Chat History. Leave chat history on so you can see your previous conversations.
  5. Find the option called “Improve the model for everyone” (or similar wording) and switch it off so your chats are not used for training.

The video above demonstrates these steps. If anything looks different on your screen, just call or email us and we can help.
