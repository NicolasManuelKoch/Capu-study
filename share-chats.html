How to share four ChatGPT chats with the researchers

During the study, we ask you to share 4 ChatGPT conversations with us, one from each week of the 4-week study. Please do not include private details (for example, names, addresses, or account numbers).

This short video shows how to pick one chat from each week and copy or share it with the research team.

Step 0 – Randomly pick one day in each week

To keep things fair, we will choose one random day in each week of the study. These numbers are generated only once, so please write them down or take a screenshot before you continue.

Week 2 (Days 8–14) –
Week 3 (Days 15–21) –
Week 4 (Days 22–28) –

After the numbers appear, please note them down. Example: if Week 1 shows Day 1, look at your earliest chats in Week 1 and pick a chat from around the first day of that week.

Part 1 – Using the numbers to choose your four chats

  1. Open the ChatGPT app on your phone or tablet.
  2. Go to your list of recent chats (your history).
  3. For Week 1:
    • Look at the number shown for Week 1 above (for example, Day 1–7).
    • Scroll to the chats from the first week of the study.
    • If the number is 1, scroll all the way down to your earliest chat that week and choose the first chat.
    • If the number is larger (for example, 4), count your chats for that week and choose the 4th chat in that week. If you have fewer chats than that number, choose the last chat in that week.
  4. Repeat for Week 2, Week 3, and Week 4:
    • Use the random day shown for that week (Week 2: Days 8–14, Week 3: Days 15–21, Week 4: Days 22–28).
    • Scroll to the chats in that week.
    • Count through your chats for that week until you reach the number, and select that chat. If there are fewer chats than the number, select the last chat in that week.
  5. In the end, you will have 4 chats: one from Week 1, one from Week 2, one from Week 3, and one from Week 4.

Part 2 – How to share each chat (share the link)

For each of the 4 chats you chose, you can send us a link to the conversation.

On iPhone / iPad

  1. Open the chat you want to share.
  2. At the top of the screen, tap the three dots (⋯) or the share button.
  3. Choose Share chat (or similar option).
  4. In the menu that appears, tap Copy link.

On Android phone / tablet

  1. Open the chat you want to share.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) or the share button at the top of the screen.
  3. Choose Share chat (or similar option).
  4. Select Copy link from the list.

Repeat these steps for each of the 4 chats (Week 1, Week 2, Week 3, and Week 4).

Step 3 – Put your four links together and send them

Paste the link for each week in the box below (for example: “Week 1: …”, “Week 2: …”), then press Submit links. This will open our secure survey page, where you can paste and submit your links.
