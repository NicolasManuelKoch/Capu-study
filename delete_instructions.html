How to delete your ChatGPT account

If you decide that you no longer want a ChatGPT (OpenAI) account after the study, you can permanently delete it. The video below shows the process step by step for the ChatGPT website and the mobile apps.

This video shows how to open your ChatGPT account settings on the website or mobile app and use the Delete account option to permanently remove your account.

Quick steps (summary)

  1. Open ChatGPT (on the website or in the app) and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to your Settings.
  3. Open the section called Account or Data Controls.
  4. Choose Delete account.
  5. Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

On the ChatGPT website (computer, tablet, or browser on your phone)

  1. Go to chatgpt.com (or chat.openai.com) and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Click Settings.
  4. In Settings, click Account.
  5. Under Delete account, click Delete.
  6. Follow the on-screen steps to confirm and permanently delete your account.

In the ChatGPT iPhone or iPad app

  1. Open the ChatGPT app and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Open your account settings (tap your name or profile).
  3. Tap Data Controls.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete Account again.

In the ChatGPT Android app

  1. Open the ChatGPT app and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Tap the menu icon to open the side menu.
  3. Tap your name to open Settings.
  4. Go to Data Controls.
  5. Tap Delete Account, then confirm the deletion when asked.

If you need help at any point, call us at (236)808-4099 and we can guide you through the steps.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your ChatGPT / OpenAI account is permanently deleted. You won’t be able to sign in with that account again.
  • Any active paid subscription (e.g., ChatGPT Plus) linked to that account is cancelled as part of the deletion process.
  • Your chats, custom instructions, and other account-level data are removed according to OpenAI’s data-retention policies.
  • If you ever want to use ChatGPT again in the future, you may need to create a new account, possibly with a different email address.

For the purposes of this study, you do not have to delete your ChatGPT account. Simply removing the app from your phone after the study is enough. Deleting your account is optional and only needed if you personally wish to stop using ChatGPT altogether.
