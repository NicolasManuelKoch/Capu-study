How to turn off ChatGPT data training (keep history on)

These steps show you how to turn off the setting called “Improve the model for everyone” in ChatGPT. This means your new chats won’t be used to train the model, but your chat history will stay on and you can still see your past conversations.

This short video shows how to find your Data Controls and turn off “Improve the model for everyone” in the ChatGPT app.

What does “Improve the model for everyone” do?

When it is on
Your conversations may be used to help train and improve ChatGPT for all users.
When it is off
Your new chats are not used to train ChatGPT, but they can still appear in your chat history like normal.

For this study, we recommend turning “Improve the model for everyone” off so your study chats are not used for training.

Step-by-step – Turn off training in the ChatGPT app (iPhone / Android)

The steps are very similar on iPhone and Android. The screens may look slightly different, but the names are the same.

  1. Open the ChatGPT app on your phone.
  2. Tap your profile icon or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Tap Data Controls.
  5. Find the option called “Improve the model for everyone”.
  6. Tap the switch to turn “Improve the model for everyone” off.
    • If a message pops up explaining the change, read it and tap Turn off or Confirm.
  7. That’s it — your chats from now on will not be used for training, and you can still see your chat history as usual.

This change only affects training. It does not delete or hide your past chats.

Checking and getting help

How to double-check the setting
You can return to Settings → Data Controls any time to make sure that “Improve the model for everyone” is still turned off.
